What I do

🛒 Marketplaces live or die by the flywheel. Differentiated supply pulls in demand. A great product keeps them. Word of mouth compounds. When sellers make money, more sellers show up, and around it goes. 🔁

Most marketplaces don’t fail because the model breaks. They fail because the product gets tired. 😴 The interface bloats, the experience slows down, and someone cleaner and faster eats their lunch.

When growth slows, the reflex is to squeeze the lemon. 🍋 Take a bigger cut. Add fees. Push conversion harder.

That works for a quarter, and it hurts for years.

The better move is a better product. ✨ First, a core experience so good there’s no reason to leave. Then extend beyond the initial match: give both sides reasons to keep working on the platform, not around it. Insurance, services, tools, add-ons. Utility that makes the whole job easier, keeps the relationship on-platform, and quietly grows revenue. That’s what earns repeat use, and repeat use is what brings the next customer in the door.

I help founders build the simple, well-made core that keeps the flywheel spinning, and figure out what’s worth adding around it. 🛠️ That’s true whether you’re starting cold or defending something mature.

I work fractional or by the project, usually three to six months. 🤝
